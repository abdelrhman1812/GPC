ABOUT US

We Are Specialized In Prosthetic Eye Care

General Prosthetic Center was established in the year 2020 with a passion to serve the community bringing life changing prosthetic procedures especially of the eye.

Our patients are generally those who went through the trauma of losing an eye due to accidents or various illness. We strive by working with attention to detail on giving back the appearance of those subjects who already had undergone surgical procedures in eye hospitals.

General prosthetic center is run by an experienced craftsman with international exposure. The lab is equipped with all modern equipments and quality material is used in all procedures.

First Visit

What to Expect During Your First Visit

1. Consultation

Your journey begins with a consultation where we discuss your needs, concerns, and expectations. This is your opportunity to ask questions and understand the process.

2. Evaluation

Our ocularist will examine your eye socket, take precise measurements, and discuss preferences regarding the color and appearance of the artificial eye.

3. Customization

Using the latest technology and materials, we create a prosthetic eye that matches your natural eye as closely as possible.

4. Fitting

Once your prosthetic is ready, we ensure a perfect fit and make any necessary adjustments for maximum comfort and aesthetics.

5. Education

We provide guidance on maintaining and caring for your prosthetic eye, including hygiene and cleaning procedures.

6. Follow-up

Regular check-ups help ensure continued comfort and optimal fit.
